Our Client-Oriented Web Development Process

We believe that the best digital solutions are built with the client, not just for the client. Our process ensures you’re involved every step of the way, from planning to launch and beyond. The procedure may differ depending on your needs but here is an overview:

(1) System Planning & Data Flow Diagram (DFD)

Every successful project starts with a solid plan. We begin by mapping out a Data Flow Diagram (DFD)—a visual representation of how data moves through your system.
Your Role: At this stage, we work closely with you to gather insights about how your business operates. Your feedback helps shape the structure of the system before we move forward

download an example of a dfd

(2) UI/UX Design Phase

Once the structure is clear, we move on to the design phase. Our goal is to create a visually appealing, easy-to-use interface that aligns with your brand
Your Role: You’ll be involved in the design review process. We provide mockups and prototypes, allowing you to give feedback before we finalize the look.

download an example of a design

(3) Development & Implementation

With the design finalized, we move on to development. This is where your website or web app is built, ensuring it’s functional, scalable, and optimized for performance.
Your Role: We keep you updated with progress reports and test versions. You’ll have a chance to test the website before it goes live.

go to developed projects

(4) Digital Marketing & Growth

A website alone isn’t enough—it needs visibility. After development, we help you attract visitors and convert them into customers
Your Role: We collaborate with you to refine messaging, target the right audience, and track performance. Your insights help us adjust strategies for maximum impact.
