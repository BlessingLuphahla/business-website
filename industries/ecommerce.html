Frequently Asked Questions

Can I manage my own inventory and orders?

Yes. We’ll build or integrate a dashboard so you can handle stock, orders, and fulfillment easily—no coding needed.

What platforms do you build on?

We offer custom builds as well as solutions using platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or headless CMS if needed.

Is payment integration secure?

100%. We use certified, secure payment gateways like Stripe, PayPal, Paystack, Flutterwave, and more.

Can I track user behavior and sales?

Yes—we integrate Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, and more so you know exactly what’s working and where.

How much does it cost?

Starter e-commerce builds begin at $500. Advanced features, full automation, or larger product counts may raise the scope.
