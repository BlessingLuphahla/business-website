Digital Solutions for Modern Healthcare Providers

At Luxovate Digital, we help healthcare professionals take their practice online—securely, beautifully, and professionally. Whether you run a clinic, dental office, therapy practice, or private medical brand, we build full-stack solutions that earn trust and simplify patient care.

Challenges We Help Solve

  • No digital presence or an outdated website that hurts trust
  • Manual patient booking processes that waste time
  • Disorganized intake or file sharing systems
  • Non-compliance with basic privacy & data handling laws
  • Patients unable to find or reach you online

What We Deliver for Healthcare Providers

  • Clean, professional websites built for trust and conversion
  • Mobile-first design for patients on the go
  • Secure patient intake forms and document upload tools
  • Appointment scheduling integration (Calendly, Setmore, etc.)
  • Patient portal access and secure data handling
  • SEO and map optimization for local patient discovery
  • Ongoing support for compliance, updates, and hosting

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the site HIPAA or POPIA compliant?

We follow best practices for data protection and can guide you on proper handling of sensitive info. For full HIPAA/POPIA compliance, we’ll use secure forms and encrypted storage options.

Can patients upload documents or forms?

Yes. We can include file upload fields and even connect them to a secure inbox or dashboard on your end.

Do you offer calendar or booking systems?

Absolutely. We can use third-party tools or custom-built booking forms with reminders and automatic confirmations.

Can multiple doctors or staff members manage it?

Yes—we can create logins for different roles or staff members, depending on your clinic structure.

How much does it cost?

Healthcare setups start at $450. Advanced forms, integrations, or portals are scoped and quoted accordingly.
